## ğŸ“š Training Structure

### Module 1: AI Awareness for PO/PM

**Content**:

- Fundamental AI concepts (generative, analytical, predictive)
- Overview of AI tools for PO/PM
- Organizational impact of AI integration
- Basics of Prompt Engineering

**Deliverables**:

- Identification of relevant AI use cases
- Mastery of basic prompt engineering techniques

### Module 2: AI-Assisted Quality, Cost, and Timeline

**Content**:

- Improving user story and specification quality
- Optimization of estimations with historical data analysis
- Timeline prediction with predictive AI
- Risk detection and prevention
- AI-assisted business cases

**Deliverables**:

- High-quality user stories generated with AI
- AI-assisted estimations
- Complete risk analysis

### Module 3: Automation and Project Structuring with AI

**Content**:

- Mapping automatable processes
- Automation tools (Zapier, Make, Power Automate)
- Design of automated workflows
- Information structuring with AI
- Automation of reporting and dashboards

**Deliverables**:

- Functional automated workflow
- Documented and optimized process

### Module 4: AI-Assisted Decision Making

**Content**:

- Data analysis and identification of insights
- AI-assisted decision frameworks (RICE, SWOT, multi-criteria)
- Comparative evaluation of alternatives
- Predictive modeling to anticipate consequences
- Communication of complex decisions

**Deliverables**:

- Complete strategic decision analysis
- AI-assisted business case

### Module 5: AI-Assisted Meetings and Documentation

**Content**:

- Automatic meeting transcription and synthesis
- AI-powered action item extraction
- Documentation generation and maintenance
- Effective and actionable reports
- Meeting time optimization

**Deliverables**:

- AI-generated report
- Complete project documentation
